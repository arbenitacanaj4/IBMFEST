Passionate students from BME bringing IBM Qiskit Fall Fest to Budapest.

Our Mission

We are a dedicated team of students from BME, united by our passion for quantum computing and commitment to making quantum science accessible to everyone. Our goal is to create an inspiring and educational experience that celebrates 100 years of quantum breakthroughs while fostering the next generation of researchers and enthusiasts.
