Celebrating 100 Years of Quantum Science

About the Event

A student-led collaboration celebrating a century of quantum science breakthroughs

IBM Quantum Fall Fest 2025 marks a historic celebration of 100 years of quantum science, bringing together the brightest minds in quantum computing for an unprecedented two-day event. This festival represents IBM's commitment to advancing quantum education and fostering the next generation of researchers.

Anyone in Budapest is welcome to participate. Expect hands-on workshops, expert-led sessions on algorithms and applications, and networking with industry leaders and fellow enthusiasts. In addition, the second day will be dedicated to a hackathon, during which participants will receive IBM certificates of participation.
