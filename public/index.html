About the Event

A student-led collaboration celebrating a century of quantum science breakthroughs

IBM Quantum Qiskit Fall Fest 2025 marks a historic celebration of 100 years of quantum science, bringing together students, researchers, and industry leaders in Budapest for a two-day festival. It is both a tribute to a century of breakthroughs and a platform for the next generation of quantum innovators.

Beyond sessions, the event is designed as an immersive experience. Day one introduces participants to the principles and applications of quantum computing through accessible sessions led by IBM Quantum experts, the BME Mobile Communication and Quantum Technologies Laboratory and more. Day two shifts into a hackathon atmosphere, where teams tackle challenges, apply their creativity, and showcase practical solutions.

The festival reflects IBM’s commitment to advancing quantum education while welcoming participants from all backgrounds to join a vibrant community passionate about the future of quantum technologies.
